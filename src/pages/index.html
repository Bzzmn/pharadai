Events & Awards

Our CBDO, Simón Espínola, participated as a moderator in this event featuring FintechChile and Stellar, where key challenges facing the fintech industry in the context of blockchain were discussed.

Our CPO, Joaquín Farfán, participated in — and won — this event with a SocialFi platform powered by augmented reality, transforming real-world locations into on-chain digital real estate. It was an inspiring experience to dive deep into smart contracts, explore Chainlink's services, and engage with forward-thinking professionals shaping the future of Web3.
